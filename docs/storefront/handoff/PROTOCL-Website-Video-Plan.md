# PROTOCL Website Video Plan

## Purpose
This document captures the plan for creating website video assets for PROTOCL without losing the current packaging, branding, and storefront direction.

The goal is to create premium, usable website video assets first, then expand into broader ad and social motion later.

## Current Recommendation

### Primary video tool
- Higgsfield

### Supporting tools
- Midjourney for still-image moodboards, storyboard frames, and visual direction
- Figma for layout, packaging, and design-system continuity

### Why this is the current recommendation
- Higgsfield appears better suited for product ads, campaign clips, and editorial product motion
- Midjourney is stronger for still-image worldbuilding than for final website video execution
- Figma remains the source of truth for packaging direction, typography, and layout consistency

## Locked Visual Direction

- Brand: `PROTOCL`
- Tone: luxury clinical editorial
- Product format: slim cylindrical airless treatment pump bottle
- Secondary packaging: tall matte-black rectangular carton
- Finish: matte black / near-black with controlled sheen
- Typography:
  - Bodoni-style serif for the logo / wordmark
  - Graphik Compact-style condensed sans for supporting type
- Product world:
  - sharp geometry
  - restrained black-on-white system
  - premium enough for luxury beauty retail and spa/clinic environments

## Phase 1: Website Video Assets

These are the first assets to create because they are the most useful for the live site.

### 1. Homepage hero loop
Goal:
- create a short premium motion loop for the homepage hero or a top-page product section

Desired feel:
- calm
- expensive
- clinical
- editorial

Potential content:
- subtle bottle rotation
- carton and bottle reveal
- controlled lighting shifts across matte-black surfaces

### 2. Product detail page motion asset
Goal:
- create a short product-focused motion clip for the treatment PDP

Potential content:
- isolated bottle movement
- gentle push-in
- small rotational reveal of typography and icon system

### 3. Editorial support clip
Goal:
- create one slightly more dimensional, campaign-style product shot for section breaks or social proof support

Potential content:
- angled bottle + carton composition
- more sculptural lighting
- richer shadow movement

## Phase 2: Production Process

### Step 1
Lock the specific shot list before generating anything:
- homepage hero loop
- PDP bottle motion
- editorial support clip

### Step 2
Use current approved packaging imagery and Figma packaging direction as visual reference.

### Step 3
Generate in short iterations rather than trying to create all motion needs in one prompt.

### Step 4
Review each output for:
- bottle consistency
- logo correctness
- packaging silhouette consistency
- premium tone
- usability on the website

### Step 5
Select winners, then optimize them for:
- homepage section usage
- PDP placement
- performance and file size

## Phase 3: What To Avoid

- generic wellness-style beauty motion
- floating leaves, droplets, sparkles, flowers, or cliché skincare props
- overly glossy or metallic-looking bottle finish
- fake or inconsistent label text
- dramatic camera movement that feels more like an ad than a premium site asset
- motion that makes the product feel mass-market or trend-driven

## Required References Before Generation

Before video generation begins, keep these as the reference set:

- current PROTOCL website header/logo direction
- current Figma packaging exploration page
- approved matte-black bottle/carton concept
- current product imagery assets already in the theme

## Future Work

After website assets are complete, the next video layer can include:

- paid-ad cuts
- social launch edits
- founder/brand mood videos
- treatment explainer clips

That work should happen only after the core website motion system feels locked.

## Immediate Next Step

When ready to begin generation, write a single Higgsfield prompt for:

- the homepage hero loop first

That should be the first real motion asset because it has the highest website value and will help establish the brand’s motion language.
